    Frequently asked questions

    When should I start my spring seeds indoors?

    Most warm‑season crops 6‑8 weeks before last frost. Check our regional calendar.

    How do I improve clay soil without tilling?

    Top‑dress with coarse compost, gypsum, and plant deep‑rooted cover crops like daikon radish.

    What’s the best organic fertilizer for tomatoes?

    Balanced 4‑6‑3 with calcium, and side‑dress with composted poultry manure at fruit set.

    Can I grow vegetables in partial shade?

    Yes — leafy greens, herbs, and root vegetables tolerate 4‑5 hours of direct sun.
