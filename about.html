About Zafora
Leaf Atelier

Independent gardening authority · since 2018

Zafora Leaf Atelier was founded by a small collective of horticulturists, soil ecologists, and botanical writers who believe that gardening is both a science and an art. We are rooted in curiosity, and we grow by sharing what we learn. Our work is driven by a deep respect for living systems, and we strive to make ecological gardening accessible without sacrificing depth.

🌱 400+ articles 📖 12 expert guides 🧪 soil-first approach 🌍 50+ contributors
⚘ OUR MISSION · since 2018

To cultivate knowledge, not just plants.

We believe that a well-informed gardener is a more resilient gardener. Our mission is to provide researched, field-tested, and beautifully written content that helps people grow food, restore soil, and deepen their connection to the natural world. We avoid hype and trends, focusing instead on principles that endure.

Our pillars

🌿 Soil first

Healthy soil is the foundation of every garden. We prioritize biology over chemistry.

📚 Evidence-based

Every article is reviewed for accuracy, and we cite sources whenever possible.

🌱 Accessible depth

We translate complex topics into clear, practical guidance for all levels.

🔄 Regenerative ethos

Our practices aim to heal ecosystems, not just produce yields.
