Cookies Policy

Transparent, minimal, and respectful · last update April 2026

Zafora Leaf Atelier uses cookies sparingly — to enhance your experience, remember preferences, and understand how our content is enjoyed. We never sell your data. This policy explains what we use and how you can control it.

🍪 minimal cookies 🔒 no tracking for ads ⚙️ you're in control 📋 GDPR compliant
🍪 COOKIE NOTICE · effective 2026

Our approach to cookies

We use first-party cookies only. No third-party advertising, no tracking across sites. Our goal is to make your visit smooth and to gather anonymized usage statistics to improve our content. You can disable cookies in your browser at any time.

first-party anonymized opt-out

What we use

📌 Essential

Session cookies to keep you logged in (if applicable) and remember your preferences.

📊 Analytics

Anonymized tracking (via our own lightweight script) to see which articles are popular.

⚙️ Preferences

Remember your language or region settings for a tailored experience.

🚫 No marketing

We do not use cookies for retargeting, advertising, or sharing with third parties.
