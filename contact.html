Before you write — quick answers

Do you offer garden consultations?

Yes — we provide remote and on‑site consultations for home gardeners and small farms. Email us with your location and goals.

How do I submit a guest article?

We welcome pitches from experienced growers. Please send a brief outline and your background to editorial@zaforaleaf.com.

Can I visit the demonstration garden?

We host open days twice a year. Follow our newsletter for dates, or book a private tour via the contact form.

I have a question about an article — where to ask?

Use the form above, or comment directly on the article page. We read every comment and often reply within 24 hours.
